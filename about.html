A Little Bit About Me

I am a Senior at Eastern Washington University getting a General Business Management degree, minoring in Visual Communication Design. (If you have a job email me please so I may eat and buy pens.) I grew up in a rural area, which often meant having the internet going out for days at a time. People thought the hundreds of hours of doodling I did was odd, but when deprived of clumsy kitten videos a girl must keep her sanity somehow. I like an odd mix in my designs. Cute, steampunk, fantasy rpg style, ect.

I enjoy pettycoats, air conditioning, ancient history & fashions, British period dramas, RPG video/tabletop games, and the great meme providing web.
